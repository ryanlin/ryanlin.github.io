SADBOI Vision AI

Underground environments are the ultimate test of robot autonomy as nearly all outside signals are blocked. Subterranean Artifact Detection using Bag Of Interestpoints (SADBOI) combines computer vision methods with multi-class SVM's to identify artifacts in realtime (20Hz) while using less system resources. This project was developed with Team Cerberus, 1st Place winners of the DARPA SubTerranean Challenge.
